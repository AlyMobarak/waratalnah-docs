## Waratalnah: Account and Data Deletion

We believe that you should always have total control over your personal data. If you have chosen to use our optional Cloud Sync feature and created a Waratalnah account, you have the right to delete that account and all associated data at any time.

### What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

When you submit an account deletion request, the following actions are irreversible:

* **Authentication Removal:** Your login credentials (provided via Firebase Authentication) will be permanently revoked.
* **Cloud Data Erasure:** All data synced to our secure cloud database will be permanently erased. This includes your User ID, Display Name, question and test progress, experience points (XP), Coins, and records of any items purchased from the motivational shop.
* **No Recovery:** Once the deletion process is complete, we cannot restore your cloud data.

### What Happens to Your Local Data?

Waratalnah is designed to work offline. **Deleting your cloud account does not automatically delete the data stored locally on your Android device.**

* After your cloud account is deleted, your app will simply revert to its default "Offline Mode."
* If you wish to completely remove all local data, test scores, and progress from your device, you must **uninstall the Waratalnah app** or clear the app's storage in your Android system settings.

### How to Request Account Deletion

You can delete your account using either of the following methods:

**Method 1: Inside the App (Recommended)**

1. Open the Waratalnah app and navigate to the **Settings** menu.
2. Scroll down to the **Account** section.
3. Tap on **Delete Account**.
4. You will be prompted to confirm your decision. Once confirmed, your cloud data will be immediately flagged for deletion.

**Method 2: Via Email Support**
If you no longer have the app installed or cannot access your account, you can request manual deletion:

1. Send an email to **support.waratalnah@gmail.com** from the email address associated with your Waratalnah account.
2. Use the subject line: **Account Deletion Request**.
3. Please include your Display Name in the body of the email.
4. We will process your request and securely erase your cloud data within 7-14 business days, notifying you once it is complete.
